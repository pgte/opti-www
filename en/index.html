Advantages

OPTi works with passion to build your dream home, with the best quality workmanship and materials and at the best price.

Highest Quality

From thermal and energy efficiency to acoustics comfort the OPTi housing system uses the most advanced technology on the market. This guarantees that you will experience maximum comfort levels within your new home.

With an energy efficiency rating of “A” (the highest possible rating)+, the OPTi system has already gone beyond the demanding regulations that will be implemented in 2020. We are years ahead of all other competitors.

Faster

OPTi houses are built in under 12 weeks. The majority of the work is done under strict quality regulations in our controlled factory environment. The home is then assembled locally on site in your chosen location. This ensures both high quality and impressive efficiency throughout the construction process.

More economical

OPTi Houses present the best price / quality ratio on the market.

Using our high-quality modular building concept, we are able to reduce the total construction costs by around 30% when compared to traditional construction and 40% when compared to the Light Steel Frame system (LSF).

Increased Security

OPTi Houses have very high levels of safety. When compared to traditional construction methods, OPTi homes have superior resistance to fire, earthquakes and hurricane winds up to 250 km per hour.

More sustainable

Did you know that 80% of the garbage on the planet is produced by construction?  OPTi is proud to state that they do not contribute to this statistic. In direct contrast to traditional methods, our construction process does not allow for waste. As a result, resources are saved in every stage throughout the construction process. Our homes and buildings have been awarded class A Energy Efficiency Certificates and are already adapted to the demanding legislation of Horizon 2020.

Customisable

We at OPTi are happy to work with you when designing your dream home and are able to tailor it to your unique needs. You may choose any of our existing designs, take part in the designing process yourself or simply adapt our existing designs to your unique specifications.

We build your home the way you want it by eliminating all of the uncertainties in the construction process.

A Home for the Future

This is the new generation of construction technology, the result of years of research and development

Certified System

The OPTi Housing System complies with even the most demanding European and US legislations. By exceeding those standards, we are more than able to provide the best structural, architectural, safety, fire resistance, energy, acoustic and thermal efficiency guarantees possible.

All of the materials that we use are subjected to a variety of comprehensive safety tests. OPTi houses are built to withstand earthquakes, cyclonic winds, and severe atmospheric conditions.

The Construction Process

The construction system uses a combination of metal framework and composite panels. The result is a well-engineered and rigid grid-like structure that can expand both vertically and horizontally. This allows our range of OPTi buildings and OPTi houses to grow in your own time and according to your particular needs and wants.

All OPTi components are dimensioned to be constructed without the use of any machinery. This allows the building components to be easily stored and transported in "standardised" containers, and that results in additional reduction in overall costs.

Investigation

The OPTi systems are the result of several years of research and development. This R&D was done on projects that were coordinated by the team at OPTi and 

partnerships with universities and institutes including Instituto Superior Técnico, the Regional Civil Engineering Laboratiry, FUNDEC and the Madeira Interactive Technologies Institute.

You can be confident that  our Research and Development team will continue to work diligently to innovate and bring the best constructive solutions for your home.
