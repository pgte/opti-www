House Porto Santo

This is the Beach House you've always dreamed of!

Porto Santo is available in 2, 3, or 4 bedroom models and is designed to be a single level dwelling. With generous room dimensions, the house is oriented on your site to face the best view and make you a part of your surroundings.

Constructed with a porch that spans one entire side of the house, the flow of light and air adds to your enjoyment and gives a sense of overlapping interior and exterior areas.

This beautifully contemporary base model contains a master bedroom suite on one side of the house for maximum privacy and two additional bedrooms with a family bathroom on the other side.

The central space that divides the bedrooms is the heart of the home and contains a living room and an open and airy kitchen with a large adjoining dining area. The full porch, running the entire length of the house, offers additional outdoor living space for open-air sitting and al-fresco dining.

We also have other layouts for you to choose from such as the smaller 2 bedroom model or larger versions of the 3 and 4 bedroom models.
