House Tatami

A fantastic house for small urban lots, the Tatami House offers an excellent compromise between quality and price.

With 150m2 over 2 floors, the private areas on the upper level are distinctly separate from the public areas on the ground floor. This makes it perfect for a growing family.

The base model contains 3 bedrooms including a master bedroom suite with a private bathroom and an additional 2 bedrooms with a family bathroom. The master bedroom in this home is large enough to have a small sitting room area or a space for doing yoga.

The open-plan living room, dining room, and kitchen can be entered through the front entry door or the second door leading to a full garage.

Other layouts to choose from include a 4 bedroom version with more generous areas.
